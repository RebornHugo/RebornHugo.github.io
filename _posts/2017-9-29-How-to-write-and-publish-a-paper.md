# How to write A paper



```

```



> 科技论文叫Paper，少数用Artical

## Title

简明扼要，一目了然，不能笼统，不要用从句。可使用名词性短语。

> Product Design for Manufacturing and Assemble

### Capital Or Small ?

* 开头大写，专有名词大写，其余小写。（专有名词大写？）

## 作者姓名

* 按汉语顺序或加条下划线

## 工作单位

* 用拼音或英文

> Notice 
>
> 1. 写的句子首先要语法正确然后转向地道
>
> 2. 广泛使用被动句，一般就应该使用被动句，有时可用'one'不用'people'
>
> 3. 时态：
>
>    * 常用一般现在时
>    * 虚拟语气？倒装？强调句？
>
> 4. 词汇：
>
>    * 趋向于用大词
>
>      > about - approximately
>      >
>      > begain - commerce
>
>    * q趋向于不用短语动词
>
>      > take in - absorb
>      >
>      > put out - extinguish
>
>    * 趋向于用科技上常用的词
>
>      > 重点 - emphasis（不用key points）
>
>    * 广泛使用名次与介词搭配
>
>      > the **advantages** of A **over** B
>      >
>      > the response of A to B
>      >
>      > the separation of A from B
>
>    * 很少使用缩短词或口语
>
>      > lab - laboratory
>
>    * 趋向于用名词来表示动作
>
>      > 分析 - an analysis of...
>      >
>      > 研究 - a study of...
>      >
>      > 介绍 - an introduction to...
>      >
>      > a limit to = limit
>      >
>      > a description of = describe
>      >
>      > provide[give] an illustration of = illistrate
>
>    * 不要使用口语缩写，如 can not 不宜写成 can't

## Abstract

不宜过长，100-150左右

约为原文长度1%~5%（有的杂志要 3%~5%）

较conclusion更为全面

```markdown
1.background
2.motivation/challenge
3.work has been done(method/model/algorithm)
4.Results
5.impact
(background motivation) (Results impact) can be together
```

> 摘要写作三个特点：
>
> 1. 谓语动词简单而句子其他部分复杂
> 2. 多用 be 被动句
> 3. 大量使用不提及人的陈述句

注意：

1. 语法正确进而地道准确

常用句式：

1. The authors present a...principle that predict...

   The paper presents the data in terms of...

   The brief history of...is presented

2. The authors describe a configuration(构造) of...

   The paper describes the principles and techniques of...

   Performance goals and various approaches to ... are briefly described.

   简述了性能目标和达到...的各种途径

3. This paper addresses the problem of...

   本文论述...的问题

4. The first paper deals with...

   第一篇论文论述了...

5. This paper is devoted to assessment of...

   本文专论如何评估...

6. This paper is concerned with the analysis of...

   本文对...进行了分析

7. The article (looks at/ explores)...

   本文考察了...



### 介绍科技论文研讨的课题

1. The performance characteristics of ... are studied theoretically and eperimentally.

   对...的性能特征进行理论和实验研究

2. The paper examines... and consider...

   本文研究了...并考虑了...

3. The article discusses the reasons for ... and offers an insight into...

   本文讨论了...的原因并提出了对...的观点

4. The influence of ...on...is investigated.

   研究了...对...所产生的影响

5. This paper analyzes some important characteristics/fearures of...

   本文分析了... ... 的一些重要特征

### 介绍论文涉及的范围

1. Some of the specific topics discussed are :

   论述的专门课题包括...

2. The scope of the research covers...

   本研究的范围涉及...

### 综述与概括某一领域的研究课题

1. This paper reviews... summarizes the theory from ...viewpoint, discusses... and presents...

   本文综述了...，从...观点概述了这一理论，并讨论了...，还提出了...

### 介绍论文重点

1. This report concentrates on...

   本报告的重点是...

2. Attention is concentrated on...

   重点是...

3. Particular attention is/was paid to...

   特别重视...

4. The greatest emphasis has been on...

   极其重视...

   The primary emphasis in the paper is on...

   本文的重点是...

### 介绍论文的目的

1. This paper seeks to justify ... in terms of...

   本文追求的目标是从...角度来论证...

2. The aim of this study is to carry out analysis for...

   本研究的目的是对...进行分析

3. The primay objective of the study was to determine...

   本研究的主要目标是确定...

4. The work is aimed at examining...
